Pastries Menu

At "Coffee Mate?", the pastry selection is sure to impress and delight every customer.


We offer a variety of freshly baked goods, from classic croissants and buttery danishes to rich, chocolate-filled pain au chocolat and flaky almond croissants.


For those with a sweet tooth, there are decadent options like cinnamon rolls, fruit tarts, and an assortment of muffins in flavors such as blueberry, chocolate chip, and banana nut.


Savory options are also available, including cheese scones, spinach and feta pastries, and ham and cheese croissants. Each pastry is crafted with high-quality ingredients, ensuring a delicious treat that pairs perfectly with any beverage on your menu.
